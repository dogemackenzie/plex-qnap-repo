App Center Ready

One-click installs and updates via QNAP's App Center.

Add to Your QNAP NAS

  1. Open App Center > Settings > App Repository > Add.
  2. Enter Name: Plex Auto Repo, URL: https://dogemackenzie.github.io/plex-qnap-repo/repo.xml
  3. Refresh, install Plex, and stream at http://<NAS_IP>:32400/web.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this an official Plex repo? A: No, it's a community mirror—always check Plex's terms.

Q: Which QNAP models are supported? A: QTS 5.0+ on x86_64/ARM_64 NAS devices.

Q: Issues? A: Check logs at /share/PlexData/Logs or GitHub issues.
